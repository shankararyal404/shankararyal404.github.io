Traveler of Darkness | अन्धकारको यात्री

Written by: Shankar Aryal (शंकर अयार्ल) Date: 2024-11-19 Place: Kathmandu, Nepal

Traveler of Darkness

As a traveler of darkness,
I walk alone,
Searching for light,
Life's meaning and love.

In the depths of night,
Stars twinkle bright,
In the sky of my mind,
Dreams take flight.

In search of love,
I've lost myself,
But darkness taught me,
The path of self-discovery.

With every step,
Learning new lessons,
On life's journey,
I move forward.

Darkness is my companion,
Light is my goal,
This journey is eternal,
And I, a perpetual traveler.

With rays of love,
My heart illuminates,
On the path of self-knowledge,
I progress continuously.

As a traveler of darkness,
I find myself,
Life's truth and love,
This is my destination.

-Shankar Aryal

About the Poem

This poem presents life's journey through the metaphor of darkness. It encompasses themes of self-discovery, love, and spiritual awakening. Darkness is not merely the absence of light, but a symbol of life's unknown paths where we journey in search of truth and fulfillment.

Author's Message

This poem reflects the universal human experience - the search for meaning, connection, and knowledge. "Darkness" represents life's uncertainties, while the "traveler" represents all of us walking these paths.

Highlighting the power of love and the importance of self-discovery, this poem invites readers to contemplate their own life's journey.

Translation Note

This is an English translation of the original Nepali poem "अन्धकारको यात्री". While every effort has been made to preserve the essence and meaning of the original, some nuances may be lost in translation. Readers who understand Nepali are encouraged to read the original version for the full poetic experience.

Writer Reflection

This is the English translation of the Nepali poem "अन्धकारको यात्री" (Traveler of Darkness), composed on a contemplative night on November 19, 2024, by Shankar Aryal. It explores the profound journey of life through the metaphor of traveling through darkness, seeking light, love, and self-understanding. The poem delves into the universal human experience of searching for meaning, connection, and enlightenment. The "darkness" represents not just the absence of light, but the unknown paths we traverse in our quest for truth and fulfillment. Through this piece, I aimed to capture the essence of spiritual awakening and the transformative power of love and self-discovery.
