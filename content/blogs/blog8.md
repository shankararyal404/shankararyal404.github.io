            <h2>अन्धकारको यात्री</h2>
            <div class="poem-body">
                <div class="stanza">
                    <p>न यात्रा न गन्तव्य न साथी रहे</p>
                    <p>यो अन्धकारको मेजरिममा कतै हराएँ</p>
                    <p>हरेक पाइला रोकिन्छ, हरेक दिशा छलिन्छ</p>
                    <p>यो भूल र मापको सीमामा म अल्झिएँ</p>
                </div>
                <div class="stanza">
                    <p>अन्धकार पिउँछु तिम्रो आँखाबाट</p>
                    <p>मदिराको प्यालामा डुब्छ जून</p>
                    <p>न नशा उत्रन्छ, न तिर्खा मेटिन्छ</p>
                    <p>यो रात अझै गहिरिँदै जान्छ</p>
                </div>
                <div class="stanza">
                    <p>तिम्रो अनुहार खोज्दै हिँड्छु</p>
                    <p>शहरका गल्लीहरूमा</p>
                    <p>हरेक मोडमा भेटिन्छौ तिमी</p>
                    <p>तर त्यो तिमी तिमी होइनौ</p>
                </div>
                <div class="stanza">
                    <p>कुन यात्रामा हराएँ म?</p>
                    <p>न पाइला अघि बढ्छ</p>
                    <p>न पछि फर्कन सकिन्छ</p>
                    <p>बीचको यो क्षण अनन्त छ</p>
                </div>
                <div class="stanza">
                    <p>हरेक दीयो बल्छ तिम्रै आँखामा</p>
                    <p>हरेक बाटो पुग्छ तिम्रै मनमा</p>
                    <p>तर न दीयो तिम्रो हो, न बाटो</p>
                    <p>यो भ्रम पनि सत्य रहेछ</p>
                </div>
                <div class="stanza">
                    <p>अब बुझें - म हराएको होइन</p>
                    <p>हराउनु नै त पाउनु रहेछ</p>
                    <p>अन्धकारमा डुब्नु नै</p>
                    <p>उज्यालो हुनु रहेछ</p>
                </div>
                <div class="stanza">
                    <p>यो यात्रा कहाँ टुंगिएला?</p>
                    <p>शायद टुंगिनु नै यात्रा हो</p>
                    <p>र हामी सधैं हिँडिरहने छौं</p>
                    <p>यो अन्धकारको मेजरिममा</p>
                </div>
            </div>
            <div class="author">-शंकर अयार्ल</div>

                        <h2 class="section-title-w">Writer Reflection</h2>
                        <p>Writing The Traveler of Darkness (अन्धकारको यात्री) was a journey for me—one that explored the deepest aspects of the human experience. The poem is a reflection of the three types of journeys that we all undergo in life: physical, mental, and spiritual.
                            <ul>
                                <li class="writer-list"><b>The Physical Journey (The Search for Love) : </b>The lover wandering through the streets represents our universal search for connection. It’s about finding someone or something to complete us, even when it feels just out of reach.</li>
                                <li class="writer-list"><b>The Mental Journey (Self-Discovery) : </b>This journey is about shedding ignorance and discovering our true selves. It’s not about acquiring knowledge but about unlearning and finding clarity.</li>
                                <li class="writer-list"><b>The Spiritual Journey (The Quest for Liberation) : </b>The ultimate goal in the poem is liberation—the search for peace and unity, transcending dualities and finding deeper meaning.</li>
                            </ul>
                            Through this poem i wanted convey that <b>"losing and gaining are two sides of the same coin".</b> In life’s journey, losing is a form of gaining. Every loss brings us closer to something deeper and more meaningful. And this journey? It never truly ends. It's eternal. Every step we take in the search for truth, love, or enlightenment, leads to another step, and the cycle continues.
                        </p>

<script>
    function showLanguage(lang) {
        const enContent = document.getElementById('en-content');
        const npContent = document.getElementById('np-content');
        const buttons = document.querySelectorAll('.language-toggle button');
        buttons.forEach(btn => btn.classList.remove('active'));
        if (lang === 'en') {
            enContent.classList.remove('hidden');
            npContent.classList.add('hidden');
            buttons[0].classList.add('active');
        } else {
            enContent.classList.add('hidden');
            npContent.classList.remove('hidden');
            buttons[1].classList.add('active');
        }
    }
    showLanguage('en');
</script>
